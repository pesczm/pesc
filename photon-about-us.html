About us

PESC is a registered Zambian firm that specializes in providing engineering services such as solar systems, electrical consultancy, and general supply of goods and services to commercial and industrial clients. Our commitment to delivering high quality products and services has propelled us beyond expectations. We place great emphasis on our every assignment.
