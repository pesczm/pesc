FAQs

👋 Say
What services do you provide?

We specialize in electrical installations and wiring, solar energy installations, general supply of electrical equipment, and consultancy services. Our team is committed to delivering safe, reliable, and efficient solutions tailored to meet your needs.

Are your installations compliant with safety codes and regulations?

Yes, all our installations fully comply with international and local electrical safety codes. We prioritize safety in every project to ensure your property is well-protected.

What types of solar systems do you install?

We install grid-tied, off-grid, and hybrid solar systems. We recommend the best system based on your energy needs, property type, and long-term goals.

Do you offer warranties on the equipment you supply?

Yes, we provide warranties on most equipment. Warranty terms vary by manufacturer, and we ensure that all products meet high quality standards.
